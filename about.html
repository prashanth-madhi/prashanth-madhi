Hello Folks,

My name is Prashanth Madhiyazhagan and I am a Honours Computer Engineering graduate from University of Waterloo and a part-time photographer. I have a strong passion to succeed and make a difference with technology.

I was born in South India, raised in Queensland, Australia and Guelph, Canada. I currently live in Sudbury, Ontario. I enjoy traveling to different countries. I have visited New York, Washington, Florida, San Francisco, Los Angeles, Detroit, Dubai, Korea, Frankfurt and Singapore.

In addition, I enjoy music and movies from multiple languages, spending time with friends and family, playing sports, going to the gym and keeping up with the latest technical news in the industry. I enjoy meeting new people and am very passionate about expanding my social and professional network.

I used to be part of the Engineering society at University of Waterloo. I have been an Education Assistant, Class/Academic Rep and represented Computer Engineering at Welcome Week, You@Waterloo Day, Shadow Day and March Break Open House. I have been part of Ultimate Frisbee and Volleyball intermural Waterloo sports teams. I used to be an active member of University of Waterloo Indian Connection (UWIC), and Photography Club on campus.

I worked as a Project Management Co-op at Manulife. Prior, I have worked as a Project Management Co-op at Research In Motion (RIM), iPhone software intern at Apple Inc in Cupertino, California. Furthermore, I was a UW Campus Ambassador for RIM, Software Tools Specialist at RIM, Sales Associate at Future Shop and Summer Research Student at University of Guelph.

If you would like to get in touch with me please email me directly at prashanth.madhi@gmail.com. I eagerly look forward to hearing from you.

Thank you for visiting my home page.
